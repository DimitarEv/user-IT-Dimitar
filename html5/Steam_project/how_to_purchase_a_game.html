How to purchase a game ?

  1. Enter the Steam application from your browser or you'r executable file.

    2. Then go to the search tab and add the title of the game that you want to search.

    3. After you add you'r game title It will show you the result of your search.

    4. Then you can browse and click the product that you wish to inspect.

    5. After you decide that you want to purchase this product you can click the "Add to Cart" button.

    6. Then it will ask you if you'r purchasing it for youtself or for a friend

    7. After you choose one of thoese options and then it will show you the payment information.

    8. And finally click the "Continue" button to complete the purchase.

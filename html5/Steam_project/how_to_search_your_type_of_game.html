How to search your type of game ?

  1. Open your steam appiclation or web browser https://store.steampowered.com/
  2. Then you click the button "Categories". Its location is next to "New & Noteworthy" and "Points Shop"
  3. After that you can choose which "genres" you wish to check in... for exmaple "Action", "Role-Playing", "Strategy" and more
